TITLE: Selected Public Welfare Schemes
DEPARTMENT: Social Welfare Department
CATEGORY: schemes

SENIOR CITIZEN: Indira Gandhi National Old Age Pension Scheme (IGNOAPS)
provides a monthly pension to citizens above 60 years who are below the
poverty line. Apply at the local Taluk or Revenue office with age proof,
income certificate, and bank account details.

WOMEN SAFETY: The Women Helpline (181) and Women Police (1091) provide
24x7 support for harassment, domestic violence, and safety concerns. One
Stop Centres (Sakhi Centres) offer shelter, legal aid, and counselling in
most districts.

STUDENT SCHOLARSHIPS: State and central government post-matric and
pre-matric scholarships are available for SC/ST/OBC and minority students
based on family income criteria, applied through the National Scholarship
Portal.

DISABLED WELFARE: Unique Disability ID (UDID) card holders are eligible for
travel concessions, reserved employment quotas, and assistive device
subsidies through the District Disability Welfare Office.
