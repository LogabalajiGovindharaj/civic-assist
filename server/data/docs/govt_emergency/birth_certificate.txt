TITLE: Birth Certificate - How to Apply
DEPARTMENT: Municipal Administration / Civil Registration
CATEGORY: document_services

Apply online through the state Civil Registration portal, or in person at the
local Municipality or Panchayat office where the birth was registered.

Required documents: hospital discharge summary or birth intimation slip,
parents' ID proof (Aadhaar), address proof.

Processing time: 7-15 working days for a fresh certificate.

Fee: Nominal, typically under Rs. 50 if applied within 21 days of birth.
A late registration fee applies after 21 days, and after 1 year a magistrate
order or affidavit may be required.

If the online portal rejects the application, visit the Registrar of Births
and Deaths office in person with original documents.
