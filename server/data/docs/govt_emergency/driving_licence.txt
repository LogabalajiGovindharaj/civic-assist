TITLE: Driving Licence - Learner's and Permanent
DEPARTMENT: Regional Transport Office (RTO) / Ministry of Road Transport
CATEGORY: document_services

Learner's Licence (LL): Apply via the Parivahan Sarathi portal, complete the
online test, and book a slot at the RTO if a physical test is required.
Valid documents: Aadhaar, address proof, age proof, passport photo.

Permanent Licence: Can be applied 30 days after the LL is issued. Requires a
riding/driving skill test at the RTO (many RTOs now use automated tracks).

Fees: Learner's Licence fee is nominal (under Rs. 200 including test fee).
Permanent Licence fee is under Rs. 700 including smart card fee. Fees vary
slightly by state.

Renewal: Apply within 1 year before or up to 1 year after expiry without a
fresh test; beyond that, a driving test may be required again.
