TITLE: RBI Customer Compensation Policy - ATM Failed Transactions
REGULATOR: Reserve Bank of India
CATEGORY: atm_upi_disputes

If a customer's account is debited but cash is not dispensed at an ATM
("failed transaction"), the issuing bank must reverse (re-credit) the
transaction within a maximum of 5 working days from the date of the
transaction, without requiring the customer to file a claim.

Failure to re-credit within 5 working days entitles the customer to a
compensation of Rs. 100 per day of delay, credited automatically to the
account.

Process: Note the ATM location, date, time, and reference number from the
SMS or transaction slip. Raise a complaint with the card-issuing bank
(not the ATM's bank) via the helpline, app, or branch. If unresolved after
30 days, escalate to the RBI Banking Ombudsman via the CMS portal.
